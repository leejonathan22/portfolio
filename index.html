Full-stack internship CRM built to close a gap in student recruiting tools. Shipped v1, ran user research, then shipped an AI cover letter assistant and mock interview module in v2.

Next.jsSupabaseVercelAI

Most students track recruiting in a borrowed Google Sheets template. I wanted something that actually worked for me — so I built it.

v1 was a clean application and relationship tracker. You could log companies, track status, and manage recruiter contacts in one place. I demoed it to 15+ students and the feedback was consistent: the tracking was useful, but they wanted the tool to do more than organize — they wanted it to help them prepare.

v2 integrated the Claude API to add an AI assistant for coffee chat prep and interview practice. The idea came naturally — I was already using Claude to prep for my own coffee chats and interviews, so I built it directly into the product. Instead of jumping between a spreadsheet and an AI tool, students could stay in one place from application to offer.

It's the project I'm most proud of because it followed a real PM arc — identify a gap, ship fast, talk to users, iterate on signal.
